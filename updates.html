Buses still active after new buses arrive

San Francisco Muni

The 8500 series (Orion VII 32') were replaced by 30 ENC E-Z Rider II BRT units. It is unclear when the 94 hybrid and 18 electric (12 40' and 6 60') buses will arrive that will replace 112 buses in the 8600 and 8700 series. All ENC buses were delivered. Bus 8541 entered service on January 2, 2025 and 8546 on January 20, 2025.

8500 series (2007) (11/30): 8501, 8505, 8507-8508, 8510-8511, 8513, 8520, 8522, 8526, 8528, 8502
8530 series (2021-22) (27/30): 8531, 8533-8549, 8551-8555, 8557-8560

Fresno Area Express

Buses made between 2005 and 2006 (0500 and 0600 series) will be retired and were replaced into 2400 series buses.
The last remaining C40LF ran primarily on routes 33 and 34 during weekdays, but rarely during weekends.

0500 series (2006) (2/10): 0509-0510, 0508
0600 series (2006) (11/15): 0601, 0605-0606, 0608-0615

Riverside RTA

The 31300 and 31400 series (Gillig BRT Plus CNG 40') were replaced into 32300 series, which were identical with special features than the newer fleet: different wheelchair ramps, color destination signs, etc.

31300 series (2013) (8/97): 31382-31386, 31388, 31391, 31395, 31374, 31393
31400 series (2014) (5/11): 31401-31402, 31404-31405, 31409, 31403, 31407
