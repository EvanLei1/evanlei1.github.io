Latest Updates

1/28/2026:

CR → EX The last of 26 2006 Gold Coast Transit New Flyer C40LFRs were retired on December 16, 2025.

Archive

11/8/2025:

CR → EX The last of 30 San Francisco Muni 8500 series (Orion VII 07.503 buses) were retired from service on October 21, with 8528 the last unit and were replaced by ENC E-Z Rider II BRT buses in the 8530 series

10/16/2025:

CR → EX The last of 26 San Diego MTS 600 series (2011 New Flyer C40LFR) were retired from service on October 14, with 604 the last unit and were replaced by 2025 XN40s in the 300 series

02/13/2025: All of the 2013 New Flyer XN60 buses that San Diego had retired. The AC Transit 1700 series buses recently entered service, displacing most 1300 series.
12/04/2024: The Santa Clara VTA 35-foot buses made in 2002 (2101-2121) were retired. Only three 1000 series (2001 Gillig Low Floor) remain as of January 28, 2026.
07/2024: Marin Transit buses 3301-3307 (2010 New Flyer DE35LF) were retired and replaced by 4 Gillig BRT HEV 35' and 3 BRT HEV 40' buses (2401-2407). The only remaining operator (as of November 2024) using the DE35LFs were in Puerto Rico.
