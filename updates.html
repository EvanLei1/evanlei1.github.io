Updates

Buses still active after new buses arrive

Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority

The 1000, 2000, 2100, and 2200 series buses will be replaced by the new Gillig Low Floor Plus HEV 40' with the numbers 3401-3492. The highest numbered bus in service is 3483. All 92 new buses were delivered. Out of 192 old buses, 36 were still in service.
The 2100 series (2001 Gillig Low Floor 35') were retired on December 4, 2024.

1000 series (2001) (6/52): 1007, 1009-1010, 1021, 1040, 1048, 1020, 1022-1023, 1036-1037, 1042, 1046
2000 series (2001) (9/61): 2012, 2014, 2023, 2031, 2037, 2042, 2047, 2054, 2062, 2018, 2038, 2041, 2048, 2050, 2055, 2057-2058, 2060-2061, 2070
2200 series (2002) (21/56): 2215, 2218, 2221, 2224-2226, 2229-2232, 2234-2235, 2240, 2242-2245, 2247-2250, 2252, 2254

Santa Francisco Muni

The 8500 series (Orion VII 32') were replaced by 30 ENC E-Z Rider II BRT units. It is unclear when the 94 new buses will arrive that will replace 112 buses in the 8600 and 8700 series. All but one ENC buses (8550) were delivered. Bus 8541 entered service on January 2, 2025.

8500 series (2007) (9/30): 8502, 8505, 8507-8508, 8510-8511, 8522, 8526, 8528, 8501, 8513, 8520
8530 series (2021-22) (26/30): 8531, 8533-8545, 8547-8549, 8551-8555, 8557-8560

Stanislaus Regional Transit Authority

Buses made between 2003 and 2009 (203xx to 209xx series) were retired and were replaced into 223xx series buses. The only 203xx bus is 20323, a 2003 Gillig Low Floor 35' bus. The 208xx series (ex-START) retired in 2023, while the lone MCI 102-D3 bus 29914 (1999) retired in August 2024 by the 224xx series.

203xx series (2003) (1/14): 20324, 20323
209xx series (2009) (2/7): 20940, 20942, 20946

Fresno Area Express

Buses made between 2005 and 2006 (0500 and 0600 series) will be retired and were replaced into 2400 series buses.

0500 series (2006) (3/10): 0508-0510
0600 series (2006) (10/15): 0601, 0606, 0608-0615, 0605

Santa Cruz Metro

Santa Cruz Metro has a lot of buses purchased seconhand from other transit agencies. For example, four 2300 series buses were replaced by the 110xx series while the xx23 series buses replaced the 2800 series buses operating on Highway 17 Express. The 110xx series were New Flyer XN60 buses bought from San Diego MTS (only 1105, 1113, and 1121 were active as of January 12, 2025). Santa Cruz Metro permanently wrap these buses in ORAT livery and added the 0 in the middle, which were 11012, 11018-11019, 11022-11023, and 11025-11028.

There were speculations and images of buses bought secondhand by SCMTD. Several ex-OCTA New Flyer C40LFR units were discovered and were unnumbered. It was used to replace the 9800 and 2200 series, while the 15 ex-RTA 31300 series buses will replace the 2600 and 2800 series. The only 2002 D40LF bus in service retired in November 2024.

2200 series (2003) (11/28): 2210-2212, 2214-2218, 2220, 2223-2224, 2226
2300 series (2002, ex-VTA) (3/4): 2316, 2318, 2333 2600 series (2006) (2/2): All buses in service
2800 series (2008) (13/13): All buses in service
9800 series (1998) (7/30): 9801, 9810-9811, 9816-9817, 9820, 9824, 9827

AC Transit

The 1200 series were retired in early 2024 by the late 1600 series. The 6000 series were replaced by the 6300 series by late 2023. The 5100 series were active, but I don't know when it will be replaced. Most 5100 series were retired in 2023, while the 1200 series retired in late 2023.

2190 series (2009) (9/9): All buses in service
5100 series (2008-09) (5/39): 5118, 5120-5123, 5130-5132

Riverside RTA

The 31300 and 31400 series (Gillig BRT Plus CNG 40') were replaced into 32300 series, which were identical with special features than the newer fleet: different wheelchair ramps, color destination signs, etc.

31300 series (2013) (10/97): 31374, 31382-31386, 31388, 31391, 31393, 31395, 31373
31400 series (2014) (7/11): 31401-31405, 31407, 31409
