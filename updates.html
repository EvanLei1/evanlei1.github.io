Buses still active after new buses arrive

Fresno Area Express

Buses made between 2005 and 2006 (0500 and 0600 series) will be retired and were replaced into 2400 series buses.
The last remaining C40LF ran primarily on routes 33 and 34 during weekdays, but rarely during weekends.

0500 series (2006) (3/10): 0509-0510 0508
0600 series (2006) (11/15): 0601, 0605-0606, 0608-0615

Riverside RTA

The 31300 and 31400 series (Gillig BRT Plus CNG 40') were replaced into 32300 series, which were identical with special features than the newer fleet: different wheelchair ramps, color destination signs, etc.

31300 series (2013) (8/97): 31382-31386, 31388, 31391, 31395
31400 series (2014) (5/11): 31401-31402, 31404-31405, 31409
