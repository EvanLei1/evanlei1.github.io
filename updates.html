Archive

02/13/2025: All of the 2013 New Flyer XN60 buses that San Diego had retired. The AC Transit 1700 series buses recently entered service.
12/04/2024: The Santa Clara VTA 35-foot buses made in 2002 (2101-2121) were retired. Only three 1000 series (2001 Gillig Low Floor) remain as of February 8. Most of the 3400 series buses were delivered, with the highest being 3489.
11/20/2024: A missing person named Eva Eddington, whom I recognized her at the Boys and Girls Club of San Francisco. She was found safe two weeks later.
07/2024: Marin Transit buses 3301-3307 (2010 New Flyer DE35LF) were retired and replaced by 4 Gillig BRT HEV 35' and 3 BRT HEV 40' buses (2401-2407). The only remaining operator (as of November 2024) using the DE35LFs were in Puerto Rico.
