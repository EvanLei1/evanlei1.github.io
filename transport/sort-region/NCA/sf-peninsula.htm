Buses to Look Out for

This section was updated on Sunday, August 10, 2025. The next update will be on August 25.

The 8500 series (Orion VII 32') were replaced by 30 ENC E-Z Rider II BRT units. It is unclear when the 94 hybrid and 18 electric (12 40' and 6 60') buses will arrive that will replace 112 buses in the 8600 and 8700 series. All ENC buses were delivered and in service. Bus 8541 entered service on January 2, 2025, 8546 on January 20, 2025, 8550 on February 26, 8556 on April 30, and 8532 on July 15.
