Buses to Look Out for

This section was updated on Saturday, April 11, 2026. Active and inactive buses were retrieved from Pantograph and Transsee.
Fleet information was provided by Wikipedia and CPTDB.

The 8500 series (Orion VII 32') were replaced by 30 ENC E-Z Rider II BRT units. It is unclear when the 94 hybrid and 18 electric (12 40' and 6 60') buses will arrive that will replace 112 buses in the 8600 and 8700 series. All ENC buses were delivered and in service. Bus 8541 entered service on January 2, 2025, 8546 on January 20, 2025, 8550 on February 26, 8556 on April 30, and 8532 on July 15.
The last remaining Orion VII (8528) ran on 37 Corbett on October 21, 2025.
Last active LRV3 train: car 1550 running on J Church on the night of October 11-12. It retired on November 12 after a farewell trip.

The 1000, 2000, 2100, and 2200 series buses will be replaced by the new Gillig Low Floor Plus HEV 40' with Allison eGen Flex hybrid system as 3401-3492. All 92 new buses were delivered and entered service. Out of 192 old buses, 13 were still in service. Note: Training buses were labeled in black, while active buses were labeled in red.
