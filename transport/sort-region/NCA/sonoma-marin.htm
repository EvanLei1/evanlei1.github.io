Petaluma Transit

Most of the fleet were made up of Gillig BRT vehicles from 2007 to 2016 (6 35', 4 29', and 1 40'). Three 1998 New Flyer D40LF buses purchased from Santa Rosa CityBus in 2014 were here, and only bus 43 was active.

Sonoma County Transit

Orion, ENC, and BYD buses make up the fleet. It consists of 14 Orion VII NG CNG buses made in 2010 and 2012, 17 ENC Axess CNG 40' from 2014 to 2019, 7 ENC EZ Rider II BRT CNG 32' buses made in 2015, 2017, and 2019, and 9 BYD buses of various length made from 2018 to 2023. The last remaining Orion V CNG buses retired in 2024.

Santa Rosa CityBus

Most of the buses were New Flyer, including 7 2011 New Flyer XDE40 buses and 4 Proterra ZX5 40'. Four 2018 ENC Axess BRT 40' were used in service. There were some 2002 Gillig Low Floor 29' and 40' and 2008 Gillig BRT HEV 29' buses in service. Two buses were seen on Pantograph as 2024 models, but the model is unknown.

Buses to Look Out for

For Petaluma Transit, all 3 1998 New Flyer D40LF buses were inactive since February. Sonoma County Transit Orion V CNG units retired in September 2024. All of the 2010 and some 2012 units were used for contingency fleet for the Golden Gate Transit.
