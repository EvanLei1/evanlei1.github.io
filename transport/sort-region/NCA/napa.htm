Solano and Napa Bus List

Solano County

Soltrans

Old buses

  • 21 2011 Gillig Low Floor HEV buses in the 3900 series

Acquired buses

  • Bus 4100 as a 2016 Nova Bus LFS CNG unit
    • Used to be San Antonio VIA Metropolitan Transit pilot unit 421
    • Only Nova Bus LFS CNG unit in service in California
  • 9 MCI D4500CT buses in the 5050 series
    • Transferred from FAST in 2022

Original buses

  • 21 2017-19, 2022 MCI D4500CT CNG buses in the 5100 series
    • First 6 buses were made in 2017
    • 4 buses were made in 2018
    • 6 buses were made in 2019
    • 5 buses were made in 2022

Electric buses

  • 4 2017, 2019 BYD K9M buses in the 4500 series
    • 2 buses were made in 2017, while the other two were made in 2019
  • 4 or 5 2025 Gillig Low Floor Plus EV 40' buses in the 4600 series
  • Bus 5501 as a 2022 BYD C10M coach

Fairfield-Suisun Transit

Serves the city of Fairfield and Suisun City. The entire fleet is run by Gillig BRT units, with 3 electric 35-foot vehicles.

Vacaville City Coach

Serves the city of Vacaville. The entire fleet is made by New Flyer.

Napa County

Napa Valley Transit

Old buses

  • 4 2013 ElDorado National Axess BRT 40' buses starting at 135
    • Suburban configuration
  • 6 2013 ElDorado National Axess BRT 35' buses starting at 139
  • 5 2013 ElDorado National Axess BRT 35' CNG buses in the 200 series

Acquired buses

  • 6 2016-17 ElDorado National E-Z Rider II BRT 32' CNG buses starting at 205
    • Used to run at Los Angeles World Airports, primarily in LAX
  • Bus 259 as 2016 ElDorado National Axess BRT 40' unit
    • Formerly a demo from the bus manufacturer with a Luminator color sign
    • Has CNG roof fairings, but it has a diesel engine
  • 4 2019 Gillig Low Floor HEV 40' buses starting at 1964
      • Leased from Golden Gate Transit of the same numbers

Original buses

  • 7 2016 ElDorado National Axess BRT 40' buses in the 250 series
    • First 5 units uses regular seating
    • Buses 257-258 have suburban seating
  • 2 2016 ElDorado National Axess BRT 35' buses starting at 255

Electric buses

  • 5 2022 BYD K7M-ER buses in the 300 series
  • About 10-15 2025 Gillig Low Floor Plus EV 40' buses in the 330 series
  • 15 2025 Gillig Low Floor Plus EV 40' buses starting at 305
