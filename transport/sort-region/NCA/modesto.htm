Blossom Express

There is a single fixed route from Modesto to the city of Ripon using 2 Gillig BRT Plus CNG 35' buses made in 2012 and 2018 respectively.

Manteca Transit

Serves the city of Manteca. There were Ford and Chevrolet buses as well as one Gillig Low Floor CNG 35' bus and two 29' buses.

Tracer

Serves the city of Tracy. There were six lettered routes around the city using 5 2017 Gillig BRT 35' and 4 2021 29' buses.

Grapeline

Serves the city of Lodi. Four 2024 Gillig Low Floor CNG 29' buses replace the 2012 ElDorado National E-Z Rider II BRT buses. One 2019 Gillig Trolley Replica CNG 35' bus was also on the road along with Ford and Chevrolet buses.

Buses to Look Out for

This section was updated on Tuesday, September 30, 2025. The next update will be on October 15, 2025.

Buses made between 2003 and 2009 (203xx to 209xx series) were retired and were replaced into 223xx series buses. The only 203xx bus is 20324, a 2003 Gillig Low Floor 35' bus. The 208xx series (ex-START Orion VII NG CNG) retired in 2023, while the lone MCI 102-D3 bus 29914 (1999) retired in August 2024 by a new MCI bus.
The oldest buses ran weekdays, and it is rare to find the oldest bus during the weekends.
