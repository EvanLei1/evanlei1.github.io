Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority Bus List

There is only one transit agency for the entire Silicon Valley. Free shuttles serving Mountain View and Palo Alto do not count.

Active Fleet

Year Fleet numbers Model Notes
2000-2001 1001-1052 Gillig Low Floor 40' Only three units remain (1007, 1009, and 1010)
2001-2002 2011-2071, 2201-2256 Gillig Low Floor 40' 29 units remain, of which nine were in the 2000 series
2010-2011 0130-0199 Gillig Low Floor HEV 40' First hybrid buses for VTA with Allison hybrid transmission
2012, 2014, 2016 0201-0220, 4201-4220, 6201-6210 Gillig Low Floor HEV 40' Express buses with suburban configuration and one door.
Buses 4201-4210 were sold to Santa Cruz Metro. 2016 models have a different livery.
2014 4101-4138 Gillig BRT HEV 29' Single door units, Allison hybrid system

2023-2024 3401-3492 Gillig Low Floor Plus HEV 40' Uses an Allison eGen Flex hybrid system

Buses to Look Out for

The 1000, 2000, 2100, and 2200 series buses will be replaced by the new Gillig Low Floor Plus HEV 40' with Allison eGen Flex hybrid system as 3401-3492. The highest numbered bus in service is 3490. All but three of 92 new buses were delivered. Out of 192 old buses, 27 were still in service.

1000 series (2001) (3/52): 1007, 1009-1010
2000 series (2001) (7/61): 2012, 2031, 2042, 2060, 2062, 2069-2070, 2014, 2018, 2038, 2041, 2047, 2050, 2055, 2057-2058, 2061, 2070
