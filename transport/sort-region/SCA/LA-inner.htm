    • Still uses the color Luminator signs
  • 550 2008-2013 NABI Metro 45C CNG buses

Antelope Valley Transit Authority

Serves the cities of Palmdale and Lancaster, with commuter services to Los Angeles. All buses in this area were operated by BYD buses, while commuter services use MCI electric buses, making it a 100% electric fleet.

Burbank Bus

Most of these buses were operated with ENC Axess 35' CNG buses and 5 rare NABI Ultra LF CNG buses.

City of Commerce Municipal Bus

There were a few MCI buses an one Ford bus in service. Most of the fleet came from NABI, New Flyer, or Gillig.

City of Santa Clarita Transit

Most of the bus service uses Gillig Low Floor CNG buses, with commuter ones using MCI buses and 2 NABI 60-BRT CNG buses. Two trolley replicas were used in this fleet.

Culver CityBus

All of the fleet were New Flyer buses, running in Culver City and LAX.

Glendale Beeline

All 8 buses were ENC buses, high and low floor, operating in the city of Alhambra.

GTrans

All 8 buses were ENC buses, high and low floor, operating in the city of Alhambra.

Los Angeles County Department of Public Works

Uses ENC buses for service for unincorporated areas of the county

Los Angeles Department of Transportation

Long Beach Transit

Serves cities in south LA County. Notable units include Gillig BRT Plus CNG, BYD K9M, New Flyer XN40, XE35, XE40, GE40, and GE40LFA buses. There were 5 BYD C10M buses for commuter services and special events, and the New Flyer GE40LFA is the only operator with the type and the only LFA bus in the state.

Montebello Bus Lines

All of the buses were New Flyer, with a few gasoline electric units, and newer buses have Axion color destination signs.

Norwalk Transit System

Most of the buses were Gillig Low Floor CNG except for some old New Flyer gasoline electric buses.

Pasadena Transit

Serves the city of Pasadena. There is a mix of ElDorado National E-Z Rider II CNG buses and New Flyer XN35 and XE35 buses, with a few Ford buses.
