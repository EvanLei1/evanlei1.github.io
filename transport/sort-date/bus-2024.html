April 20

Final BART legacy train run

On April 20, 2024, Bay Area Rapid Transit held a special event commemorating the final three train sets made by Rohr between 1971 and 1979, which include a special route from Oakland's MacArthur and Fremont stations. It is a unique experience for me, as I rode the second train. The second final train arrived at 3:09 pm, and the final train arrived at 3:25 pm. The final train left Fremont station at 3:35 pm.

I was in car 1584 on the second set. The first set is reserved because it was a five-car formation, while the last two sets were available for the general public. In MacArthur, special events were held, like the speeches and the usage of the front end of a preserved BART A cab and train controls. I did not take a selfie in this event. After this event, a few legacy trains were preserved.
