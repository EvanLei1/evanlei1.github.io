Buses still active after new buses arrive

Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority

The 1000, 2000, 2100, and 2200 series buses will be replaced by the new Gillig Low Floor Plus HEV 40' with the numbers 3401-3492. The highest numbered bus in service is 3470. All 92 new buses were delivered. Out of 192 old buses, only 56 were still in service.

1000 series (2001) (14/52): 1007, 1009-1010, 1020-1023, 1034, 1036-1037, 1040, 1042, 1046, 1048
2000 series (2001) (18/61): 2012, 2014, 2018, 2023, 2037, 2038, 2041-2042, 2047, 2048, 2050, 2054, 2055, 2058, 2060-2062, 2070
2100 series (2002) (3/23): 2106, 2109, 2113
2200 series (2002) (21/56): 2215, 2218, 2221, 2224-2226, 2229-2232, 2234-2235, 2240, 2242-2245, 2247-2250

Stanislaus Regional Transit Authority

Buses made between 2003 and 2009 (203xx to 209xx series) were retired and were replaced into 223xx series buses. The only 203xx bus is 20323, a 2003 Gillig Low Floor 35' bus. The 208xx series (ex-START) retired in 2023, while the lone MCI 102-D3 bus 29914 (1999) retired in August 2024 by the 224xx series.

203xx series (2003) (1/14): 20323
209xx series (2009) (3/7): 20940, 20942, 20946

Fresno Area Express

Buses made between 2005 and 2006 (0500 and 0600 series) were retired and were replaced into 2400 series buses.

0500 series (2006) (3/10): 0508-0510
0600 series (2006) (10/15): 0601, 0605-0606, 0608-0609, 0611-0615

AC Transit

The 1200 series were retired in early 2024 by the late 1600 series. The 6000 series were replaced by the 6300 series by late 2023. The 5100 series were active, but I don't know when it will be replaced.

2190 series (2009) (7/9): 2191-2195, 2198-2199
5100 series (2008-09) (6/39): 5118, 5120-5123, 5130
